About Us

Give Light to Millions of People


Even in this technologically developing world we still have people striving to fulfill their basic needs. Our initiative is to set up a donation interface between the donors and the volunteers. Now the NGOs can easily identify appropriate donors who meet their requirements and assist in moving funds to the needy. This makes donation process straight-forward and convenient, thus increasing the amount of donations made in the society.
